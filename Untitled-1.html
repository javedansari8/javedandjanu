Welcome to Catalyst Market

Curated for Good

Catalyst Market is a collaborative project of Catalyst 2030 to connect conscious makers with shoppers like you. The marketplace features products of leading social enterprise brands from around the world.

Shared Values

Market members are offered free curation for their products, helping to ensure a high standard of sustainability and meeting certain standards across products. The platform is set up as a self sustaining, social enterprise model.

Meet the Makers

We connect shoppers directly to makers from around the world through the marketplace, expanding the makers’ audience for sustainable, handmade and culturally rich products.
